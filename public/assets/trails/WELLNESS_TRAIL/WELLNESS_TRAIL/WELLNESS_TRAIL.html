Wellness

"The WELL certification is designed to acknowledge buildings which focus on the well being of occupants and their surroundings, through the design and construction process. The WELL Building Standard is organized into eight categories: Air, Water, Nourishment, Light, Fitness, Comfort, Mind and Innovation. SDE4 is a WELL certified building, that is focused on increased well-being and productivity of its occupants through providing healthy and more active workplaces.
