BIO RETENTION BASIN

At SDE4, one-third of the rainwater runoff from the roof is conveyed into the bioretention basin located in the sorrounding landscaped area. The basin slows down the run-off, embellishes the landscape and filters water through a dense vegetation layer. The resulting harvested water is then used to meet irrigation and other non-potable water demands of the building.
